Getting Here
Free Airport Shuttle 🚌

A free circulation bus connects the airport to our building. Pick your terminal below.

SHUTTLE ROUTE OVERVIEW
FROM T1 Terminal 1 → Us
  1. Go to 3F (Departures), Gate 3 or 12.
  2. Take circulation Bus No.03 (bound for AICC).
  3. Get off at the 1st stop — Grand Hyatt Hotel.
  4. Cross the crosswalk — IBC World Gate is right there.
🧳 Walking from T1 is possible (~15 min), but not recommended with luggage.
FROM T2 Terminal 2 → Us
  1. Go to 3F, Gate 7.
  2. Take the same Bus No.03.
  3. Get off at the 4th stop — Grand Hyatt Hotel.
  4. Walk slightly in the bus's direction, then cross the crosswalk.
TO AIRPORT Us → Terminal
  1. Exit the main entrance and cross toward the Incheon Airport Corporation building.
  2. Walk right to the nearest bus stop.
  3. Take Bus No.04.
  4. T1 ~5 min (2 stops) · T2 ~25 min (4 stops).
